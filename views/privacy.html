Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

1. Who We Are

Adefem Limited (adefemcollections.co.uk) provides beauty and lifestyle services including salon bookings and product orders. Your privacy is important to us.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone, username
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials (securely transmitted)
  • Order & Booking Info: Appointments, services, orders
  • Device Info: IP address, browser type, and session data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To log you in and manage your account
  • To allow booking and purchase operations
  • To improve our services and detect fraud

4. Use of External API

We securely send login data and orders to our backend API at https://adefemecommerce.onrender.com. This is operated by our internal team and no third-party service accesses your personal data.

5. Cookies & Storage

We use localStorage and sessionStorage to manage login state and session security. You can clear this in your browser settings.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. Your data stays between you and Adefem Limited.

7. Security

All user data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and stored securely. Passwords are not stored in plain text.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

8. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction or deletion
  • Account removal

Email us at info@adefemcollections.co.uk.
